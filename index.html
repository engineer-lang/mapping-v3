Proposed Structural Changes

The following changes are proposed to improve navigation clarity and create a more intuitive information architecture.

1️⃣
Rename "Learn" → "Resources"
Navigation Menu Change
RENAME
❌ Before (Current)
  • Learn (main menu)
  • Resources → Blog
  • Resources → FAQs
  • About Always.bank
  • Meet The Team
  • Core Values
✅ After (Proposed)
  • Resources (main menu)
  • Blog
  • FAQs

💡 Rationale: "Resources" is more direct and industry-standard. About content moved to its own menu.

2️⃣
New "About" Main Menu
New Top-Level Section
NEW
❌ Before (Current)
  • About pages nested under Learn
  • Meet The Team — sub-page of Learn
  • Core Values — sub-page of Learn
  • ⚠️ Hard to find for users looking for company info
✅ After (Proposed)
  • About (new 4th main menu)
  • About Always.bank
  • Our Team
  • Our Values

💡 Rationale: Gives company identity a prominent position. Standard across banking sites.
